About Project Nethsuwa

"Bringing Vision to Every Corner"

Who We Are

We are the Women Empowerment Avenue of the Leo Club of University of Kelaniya, working on community-based initiatives that promote social well-being and equal access to essential services.

Project Nethsuwa addresses key issues related to eye health, including lack of awareness, limited access to vision care, and challenges faced by visually impaired individuals.

Project Phases

A structured three-phase journey dedicated to eye wellness and community support.

Completed

Eye Screening & Awareness

We conducted eye screening and awareness programs to promote early detection of vision issues and improve understanding of eye health.

  • Silumina Dhamma School, Madapatha | 08.02.2026
  • Buthpitiya Maha Vidyalaya | 10.02.2026
  • University of Kelaniya | 04.03.2026
PHASE 02
Completed

Distribution of Free Spectacle Frames

To support individuals with vision difficulties, we distributed free spectacle frames, helping improve clarity and daily functioning.

📍 Location

  • University of Kelaniya | 29.04.2026

Completed

Donation of Audio Books & Devices
